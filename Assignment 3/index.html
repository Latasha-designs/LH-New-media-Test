Welcome to the University of Cincinnati.

The University of Cincinnati offers students a balance of educational excellence and real-world experience. UC is a public research university with an enrollment of more than 46,000 students and has been named "Among the top tier of the Best National Universities," according to U.S. News & World Report. Visit the University of Cincinnati from the comfort of your own home! Nothing beats an in-person tour of our beautiful campus, but our immersive virtual tour is the next best thing. Our virtual tour provides 360-degree video and virtual reality experiences around campus and into residence halls, classrooms and more.
